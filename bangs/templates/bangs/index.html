A quick glance over bambot:

Pairs of command-response are called bangs. They are the base of bambot's functionality.

First we have normal bangs, which are you typical user-defined command-response pairs.

We also have special bangs, which do stuff that's a bit more complicated, and come with the bot.

It'd be pretty tedious to write a whole special bang, so you can chain it with a regular bang to make it easily reusable for your chat
